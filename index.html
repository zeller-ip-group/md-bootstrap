About Us

Zeller IP Group specializes in intellectual property protection and enforcement, including all aspects of patents, trademarks and copyrights. Like you, we’re entrepreneurs, and we’re uniquely positioned to help you align your intellectual property strategy with your business goals.

Practice Areas

Patent Drafting & Prosecution

Our patent practitioners handle the initial preparation and filing of patent applications and the subsequent prosecution of the applications before the USPTO and foreign patent offices.

IP & Technology Transactions

We help our clients maximize the value of their most precious assets by combining our technical and legal expertise with a willingness to dive deeper into our client’s technology.

Trademarks & Copyrights

Our practitioners provide a full range of services relating to trademark, copyright, trade dress, and domain name matters, including strategic counseling and prosecution work.
